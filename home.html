Powering India’s Future with Clean Solar Energy

Raytouch Renewables is one of India’s emerging solar energy providers, committed to delivering high-quality roof solar panel installations for homes and businesses alike. With a growing network of happy customers across the country, we bring trusted technology, expert support, and transparent service to make your switch to solar effortless. We offer government-approved systems, flexible zero-cost EMI plans, and full-service assistance—from consultation to installation and beyond. Our goal? To make solar energy simple, affordable, and accessible for everyone.

Our Mission

To light up every home and business in India with clean, affordable, and reliable solar power. We simplify solar adoption with innovative solutions and a customer-first mindset—building a greener and more sustainable future, one roof at a time.

Our Vision

We imagine a world where solar energy is the standard, not the exception—a future where individuals and organizations have full control over their energy costs and carbon footprint.
